Trackmania

For the sleepy head at the bakc of the classroom, i will resume what it is. The TMGL is a trackmania tournament where 8 teams of 2 players compete. Those player comes from around the world but, this year, 31 % of players are french. The canadian player CarlJr will be present, if you don’t know him, he is arguably the best player of the world, he is the magnus karlsen or the michael jordan of trackamania, he already won 6 world champion title.
